Homemade Banana Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • Pinch of salt
  • Milk (quantity not specified, but enough to make custard)
  • Vanilla wafers or cookies (quantity as desired for layering)
  • 4 bananas (or adjust to preference)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Splash of vanilla extract

For the Meringue:

  • 3 egg whites
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • Splash of vanilla extract

Optional Additions/Substitutions:

  • Crushed pineapple (optional, for variation)
  • Additional vanilla wafers for garnish
